Tip #7
Keeping Subs In The Browser Window
If you have long sub menus or a scrollable page where your subs can potentially fall of the screen, use the 'Keep Subs In Window' add-on. This add-on automatically moves a sub menu back into view before it can fall of the right edge or bottom edge of the browser window.

To activate this add-on choose 'Add-Ons --> Keep Subs In Window' from the main menu.
